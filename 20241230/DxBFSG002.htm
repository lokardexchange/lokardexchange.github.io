You’ve walked the path, the way was long,

But now the light proves you were strong.

Through doubt and night, you still held true,

And every step has carried you.

The signs ahead no longer hide,

They gently turn you to the right.

The direction clears, the course is true,

And dawn now breaks in front of you.

So do not yield, nor lose your pace,

You’re nearing now that destined place.

What once was distant, faint, and far

Now glows ahead—your guiding star.

Trust still the road beneath your feet,

Its winding end is near, complete.

The dream you chased through all this way

Now waits for you just steps away.
