Class Rules and Regulations

Stand at attention when I enter the room. You will also be instructed to stand at attention when a higher-ranking individual arrives. Upon the command “Attention!”, immediately stop what you are doing, face me, and listen.

Cheating is strictly prohibited. If caught, your paper will be confiscated, and you will be referred to the disciplinary officer.

A class mayor will be elected to maintain order, relay concerns, and ensure the classroom is clean before dismissal.

All excused absences must be supported with valid documentation and presented on the next class you attend. Acceptable excuses include illness (with a medical certificate) or the death of an immediate family member (with a copy of the death certificate).

Read and follow instructions thoroughly. Stay alert for both verbal and non-verbal cues from the instructor.

Maintain a clean workspace. Properly dispose of waste. Follow all safety and emergency procedures, and observe health protocols when applicable.

You may visit during consultation hours to inquire about your grades. Requests for grade rectification will only be entertained before the final exam. Present relevant proof such as returned assignments, quizzes, or lab work.

Group collaboration is a shared responsibility. Cooperate actively. Group performance will be evaluated.

No horseplay. Handle instruments with care. Speak quietly. Always bring the required protective equipment and follow instructions diligently. Safety is a priority.

Expect an assessment during every meeting. Half the items will count as a quiz; the other half as recitation. Bring your recitation card, 0.25 sheet of yellow paper, and a black pen every session.

You are required to turn on your camera and be ready to unmute when called. Join our Discord server during scheduled class time. If I do not arrive within 10 minutes, you may leave. Our online sessions are divided into three: (1) discussion/instructions, (2) activity, and (3) reading.

Maintain silence during class. Refrain from talking to classmates unless instructed. Communicate only with the instructor. Move quietly. Only involuntary sounds such as sneezing or coughing are excused.

Always follow the given format. Include your name and date on all submissions unless otherwise instructed.

Put devices on silent mode. Smartphones are only allowed during emergencies or for verified course-related searches. You may verify class information online if needed.
